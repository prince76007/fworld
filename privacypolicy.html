PERMISSION OBTAINED FROM YOUR DEVICE

1. CONTACT ON PHONE Used for permissions to associated with accessing the contacts in the user's device. This permission allows Fworld App to recommend the user's friends in the contacts to himself so as that they can play games and have fun on Fworld App together.
2. LOCATION Used for permissions to associated with accessing user's location. This permission allows Fworld App to show the user's location in the profile and recommend interesting people aside the user's location.
3. CAMERA Used for permissions to associated with accessing camera or capturing images/video from the device. This permission allows Fworld App to capture the user's picture in order for them to be viewed in the profile, allows the user to take photos and send to friends on Fworld App, or allows the user to live stream or video call with other user they meet on Fworld App.
4. PHONE STATE Used for permissions to associated telephony features. This permission is triggered by Google Play Install referrer API, which allows Fworld App to collect information of app downloading and usage. However, we won't collect telephony information such as IMEI and device ID.
5. MICROPHONE Used for permissions to associated with accessing microphone in the user's device. The permission allows the user to voice chat with people they meet on Fworld App.
6. STORAGE Used for permissions to associated with accessing and uploading from the storage in the users device. The permission allows Fworld App to download app resources, and upload the user's photos from the gallery in the device to the profile or to friends on Fworld App.
7. ADVERTISING ID Used for permissions to associated with accessing advertising id provided by Ad mob. The permission allows Fworld App to collect the user's data of downloading and using Fworld App, so as that the user experience and the strategy of advertising Fworld App in the market can be improved.
